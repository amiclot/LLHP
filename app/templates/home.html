The Problem

Traditional Market research (Focus Groups, Interviews, Polls, Surveys) has high cost, long lead times, and inherent bias. Linear Labs solves this problem by applying Artificial Intelligence to online conversational data.

Classification

Linear Labs groups conversations together, then builds sub-levels of conversation to better understand what is driving the original topic of conversation.

At each level of conversation, Linear Labs is able to classify 375 Topics, 13 Emotions, 5 Age Groups, 3 Genders and Sentiment. This provides users granular insights into their consumers.
